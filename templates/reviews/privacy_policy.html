Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password, and optional profile information (first name, last name).
  • Content: Reviews, comments, replies, property listings, and images you post on the Platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Platform, including pages visited and actions taken.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform.
  • Process and display your reviews, comments, and property listings.
  • Communicate with you about your account and the Platform.
  • Monitor and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • Public Content: Reviews, comments, and property listings you post are publicly visible on the Platform.
  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers who assist in operating the Platform.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Anonymous Posting

You may choose to post reviews and comments anonymously. However, we still maintain internal records linking content to your account for moderation and security purposes. Your email address and account information are never displayed publicly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information.
  • Delete your account and associated content.
  • Edit or delete your reviews and comments.
  • Request information about the data we hold about you.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and improve the Platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through the Platform's contact mechanisms.
