⚠️ Critical Safety Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: All reviews, comments, and information on this Platform are based on individual user opinions and experiences. We do not verify, endorse, or guarantee the accuracy of any content posted by users. You should not rely solely on information from this Platform when making important decisions about properties, rentals, or real estate transactions.

  • Always conduct your own independent research and verification.
  • Consult with licensed real estate professionals, attorneys, or other qualified experts.
  • Visit properties in person before making any commitments.
  • Verify all information through official sources.

1. Content is Opinion-Based

All reviews and comments on this Platform represent the personal opinions and experiences of individual users. They are not professional advice, legal opinions, or verified facts. Different users may have different experiences with the same property.

2. No Professional Advice

The Platform does not provide legal, financial, real estate, or professional advice. Information on the Platform should not be used as a substitute for professional consultation. Always seek advice from qualified professionals for important decisions.

3. User Responsibility

When posting content, you agree to:

  • Post only truthful, accurate information based on your personal experiences.
  • Not post false, misleading, or defamatory information.
  • Not post content that could cause harm or safety risks to others.
  • Respect privacy and not share personal information of others without consent.
  • Not use the Platform to harass, threaten, or harm others.

4. Safety When Viewing Properties

If you are viewing properties based on information from this Platform:

  • Always visit properties with a trusted companion when possible.
  • Inform someone of your plans and location.
  • Verify property ownership and legitimacy through official channels.
  • Be cautious of suspicious listings or requests for personal information.
  • Trust your instincts and leave if you feel unsafe.

5. Reporting Safety Concerns

If you encounter content that poses a safety risk, violates our terms, or appears fraudulent, please report it immediately. We take safety concerns seriously and will investigate reports promptly.

6. Limitation of Liability

The Platform and its operators are not responsible for:

  • The accuracy, completeness, or reliability of user-generated content.
  • Any decisions or actions taken based on information from the Platform.
  • Any losses, damages, or harm resulting from use of the Platform.
  • Interactions between users or third parties.

7. Emergency Situations

If you are in an emergency situation or immediate danger, call your local emergency services (911 in the United States) immediately. Do not rely on this Platform for emergency assistance.

8. Content Moderation

While we strive to maintain a safe environment, we cannot monitor all content in real-time. We rely on user reports and automated systems to identify problematic content. We reserve the right to remove content that violates our terms or poses safety risks.

9. Your Safety is Your Responsibility

Ultimately, your safety and the decisions you make based on information from this Platform are your responsibility. Use the Platform as a tool for gathering information, but always verify important details through official and verified sources.

10. Contact for Safety Issues

If you have immediate safety concerns or need to report dangerous content, please contact us immediately through the Platform's reporting mechanisms. For emergencies, contact local law enforcement.
